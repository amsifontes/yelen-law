About the Firm

Howard Y. Yelen, Esq.

Howard Y. Yelen is the founder and principal attorney of Yelen Law Offices, P.A., a Weston-based firm dedicated to providing high-quality legal representation in immigration and traffic law.

A graduate of St. Thomas University School of Law and a member of the Florida Bar since 1996, Attorney Yelen has spent over two decades helping individuals, families, and businesses navigate the complexities of U.S. immigration law. His practice also extends nationwide in traffic defense, with particular expertise representing CDL holders and commercial trucking companies.

“We employ creative immigration strategies — seeking creative, but legal, means to get desired results. Every client is treated like family, not a case number.”

As a longstanding member of the American Immigration Lawyers Association (AILA) and a former member of the Florida Bar Grievance Committee, Attorney Yelen maintains the highest standards of professional ethics and legal excellence.
